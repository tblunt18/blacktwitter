Research at Spelman College

Black Twitter as a Powerful Platform

The idea of free expression on social media is extremely pivotal particularly for African-Americans whose voices are often silenced. A popular platform is Black Twitter - a social subculture existing within the Twitter platform. In the past few years, racial injustice has sparked vital social movements. #BlackLivesMatter started within the realm of Black Twitter, further causing us to inquire about the usefulness of the platform. Could Twitter be used as a credible medium to determine the sentiments, needs, and opinions of those existing in the Black community? What other social movements are potentially on the rise? These are questions that we aim to explore in our research. We started gathering tweets related to the Oscars Awards in 2016 as an annual case study. Visit our Research Page for more info!
